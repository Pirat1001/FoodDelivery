    Welcome / Bienvenidos!

    Elgato is a mexican restaurant where all the recipes are inspired by original mexican cuisine.

    The restaurant has two owners, brothers with mexican roots who spent their entire lives learning about the mexican food culture.

    We offer only the original taste of mexican food, our goal is to really spread the mexican food culture throughout the entire world.

    So far we've open four restaurants, all placed in USA and we're looking forward to expanding our brand in other countries!
